Legal & Consulting Services

Expert guidance in community development law and policy

Drawing on extensive experience in government, legal practice, and community development, I offer specialized consulting and legal services to nonprofits, local governments, community organizations, and developers.

Community Development Law

Guidance on federal community development programs, CDBG compliance, HOME regulations, and navigating HUD requirements. Assistance with consolidated planning, environmental reviews, and program design.

Municipal Policy & Legislation

Drafting and analysis of local ordinances, resolutions, and policy frameworks. Experience with tax incentive programs, land use regulation, affordable housing policy, and administrative procedures.

Public-Private Partnerships

Structuring and negotiating development agreements, redevelopment contracts, and public-private partnerships. Expertise in community benefit agreements and ensuring accountability in economic development deals.

Strategic Planning & Advising

Strategic counsel for nonprofits, community organizations, and local governments on community development strategy, program design, and advocacy campaigns. Data analysis and fiscal impact assessment.

Training & Education

Workshops, presentations, and training sessions on community development law, local government processes, and policy advocacy. Curriculum development and guest lectures for academic institutions.

Grant Writing & Technical Assistance

Support with federal grant applications, technical assistance for HUD programs, and capacity building for community development organizations. Experience with CDBG, HOME, and other federal funding sources.
